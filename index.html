Our Vision

Redefining What
Pool Care Means

At Kiwi Pools UK, our vision is to set the standard for modern, dependable, and high-quality pool care. We combine cutting-edge water testing technology with decades of hands-on expertise to deliver pools that are cleaner, safer, and more efficient.

Through skilled refurbishment, smart equipment upgrades, and advanced automated dosing systems, we create pools that are effortless to own and a joy to use — year after year.

What We Offer

Complete Pool
Services

From routine maintenance to complete overhauls — we deliver precision, reliability, and craftsmanship across every aspect of pool care.

01

Pool Servicing

State-of-the-art digital water testing and comprehensive maintenance visits to keep your pool crystal clear and perfectly balanced.

02

Refurbishment

Complete onsite liner installations plus a full range of safety, slatted and solar covers to restore your pool to showroom condition.

03

Heater & Pump Install

Expert installation and replacement of pumps, heaters and filtration equipment to improve efficiency and eliminate recurring issues.

04

Automated Dosing

As certified Bayrol specialists, we install advanced chlorine and salt dosing systems for effortless, precise water treatment.

05

Safety Covers

Full selection of high-quality safety, slatted and solar covers to enhance appearance, functionality and safety.

06

Tailored Service Plans

Personalised maintenance schedules designed around your pool's unique requirements, goals and seasonal demands.
